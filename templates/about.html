Our Expertise

PROVIGILANCE® Limited, established in October 2006, provides comprehensive Pharmacovigilance (PV) and Quality Assurance (QA) services. Our experience includes over 250 audits conducted to date within the GVP, GCP, and CSV areas. We specialize in:

  • Regulatory Inspection preparation and support
  • Mock system inspections and interview coaching
  • PV system audits and documentation reviews
  • Qualification and due diligence audits
  • PV Process audits
  • GCP audits and inspection preparation
  • Quality assurance system reviews and audits
  • Inspection training of employees
  • Gap analysis and (CAPA) plan generation/review
  • SOP generation and system improvement activities
  • Audit report meta-analysis
  • Data reviews and reconciliation activities
  • Safety Data Exchange Agreement (SDEA) and regulatory document generation and reviews

Who We Are

Mike Howkins, R.G.N., Pg Dip, MRQA

SUMMARY
Since moving from the healthcare sector into the pharmaceutical industry in 1995, Mike has acquired a broad range of experience in PV and Risk Management, holding a variety of senior management roles, including as EEA Qualified Person for Pharmacovigilance (QPPV). He has been an independent PVQA consultant since July 2006, founding PROVIGILANCE® Limited in October 2006.

AUDITING EXPERIENCE
Over 250 audits conducted to date within the GVP, GCP, and CSV areas.

THERAPY AREA EXPERIENCE
Over 25 years of industry experience, including work in all therapeutic areas and medical devices.

COUNTRIES AUDITED IN
Over 50 countries worldwide, including Albania, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, Japan, UK, USA, and many more.

LANGUAGE EXPERIENCE
English (native), French (conversational), Spanish (basic), German (basic).

OTHER QA/QMS EXPERTISE
Mike has been engaged in leading and supporting a variety of PV and QA projects for international pharmaceutical companies, CROs, and other service providers, including regulatory inspection preparation, mock inspections, system audits, gap analysis, SOP generation, and more.
